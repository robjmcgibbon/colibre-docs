Equilibrium abundances & cooling rates
======================================

The fractions of hydrogen and helium species, are calculated in non-equilibrium and can be found directly in the snapshot files.
Supplementary information on the equilibrium species fractions (incl. ion fractions) and equilibrium cooling rates is also available.

The fractions of all 157 species from the chimes network, pretabulated assuming steady-state chemistry and ionization equilibrium, can be found at

``/cosma8/data/dp004/colibre/Tables/equilibrium_rates_and_speciesfractions``

Use this, for example, to find out how much carbon is in CIV, or how much oxygen is in OVI for each gas particle.
There is a ``README`` file in the same folder with more information. Feel free to contact Sylvia Ploeckinger with any questions.
