Observer positions
==================

The number and position of the lightcone observers depends on the
simulation box size:

  * In the 2.8Gpc and larger boxes, we place eight observers in the
    simulation volume at positions half way between the box centre and
    the corners.
  * In the 1Gpc boxes, we place two observers at points half way
    between the centre of the box and two diagonally opposite corners.
