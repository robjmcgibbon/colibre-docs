Missing satellite galaxies
==========================

.. _issues_lightcone_satellites_images:

Figures related to :ref:`issues_lightcone_satellites`.

The fraction of haloes in the L1_m8 and L1_m9 simulations at redshift :math:`z=0` that do not contain any bound black hole particle, as a function of bound stellar mass (first figure) and as a function of total bound mass (second figure). The fraction is shown for all haloes (solid lines) and satellite haloes only (dotted lines). Haloes without a black hole will be missing from the lightcone halo catalogues. The black lines in the second figure indicate the FoF masses at which black holes are seeded.
