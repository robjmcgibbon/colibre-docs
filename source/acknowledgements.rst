How to acknowledge use of this service
======================================

Publications making use of the public FLAMINGO data are kindly requested to:

  * Cite the two papers presenting the FLAMINGO project: `Schaye et
    al (2023)
    <https://ui.adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/2023MNRAS.526.4978S/abstract>`__
    and `Kugel et al (2023)
    <https://ui.adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/2023MNRAS.526.6103K/abstract>`__,
    as well as the data release paper (TODO: add a link to the data release paper).
  * When referring to specific aspects of the methodology, cite the original papers presenting those aspects rather than the overview paper.
  * Add the following statement to the acknowledgements:

      *We acknowledge the Virgo Consortium for making their simulation data available.
      The FLAMINGO simulations were performed using the Durham Memory Intensive system
      managed by the Institute for Computational Cosmology on behalf of the STFC
      DiRAC facility (www.dirac.ac.uk).*
